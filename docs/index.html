The Challenge

Retail grocery networks face a perfect storm: exploding data volumes, AI-driven bandwidth demands, hybrid infrastructure complexity, and cloud costs that can spiral out of control overnight.

Hybrid Infrastructure Sprawl

Multiple data centers plus Azure creates routing complexity. Every pathway decision impacts cost and performance. Without unified visibility, you're flying blind.

AI Workload Bandwidth Explosion

AI isn't a hockey stick—it's nearly vertical. 400G circuits today, terabit tomorrow. Your network architecture must anticipate what's coming, not just what's here.

Cloud Cost Escalation

"Cloud costs skyrocket fast if you don't watch it." AI workloads in public cloud can become unbearable. Ingress/egress fees punish data movement. Colo and on-prem options need evaluation.

Data Governance & Ownership

When data leaves your premises—who owns it? Where does it sit? Whose regulatory environment applies? These aren't theoretical questions anymore.

Data Silos & Integration Brittleness

Nine different hops for one dashboard. Schema changes break downstream reports. ETL/ELT complexity that no one wants to touch. Sound familiar?

The Coming AI Silos

Beyond data silos lies a new trap: AI silos. Every vendor building their own AI stack. Fragmented intelligence. Redundant spend. It's coming faster than you think.
